Buy

We buy properties in all condition (as-is) in King, Snohomish, and Pierce County - quick closing, all cash, no real estate agent commissions or fees, no "for sale" sign in your yard, and no need to make any repairs or cleaning.

Sell

We sell properties that we have meticulously remodeled - at fair market price and quality renovations that we stand by.

Pay

We pay you a "Finder's Fee" if we purchase a property thanks to you - get extra income when you refer and we close on deals from your friends, family, or your neighbors!

Relationship

We build relationships with other investment partners to continually learn and grow our business - always seeking new opportunities.

Join us

We welcome all private lenders to join us in the success of our business - diversify your investment portfolio and seek a higher ROI than conventional investments!
